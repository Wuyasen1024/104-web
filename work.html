We are seeking a talented and creative UI/UX Designer to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will be passionate about creating user-centered designs and delivering exceptional digital experiences. You will work closely with product managers, developers, and stakeholders to design intuitive interfaces and optimize the user experience across web and mobile platforms.

Key Responsibilities:
  • Conduct user research, create user personas, and define user journeys to inform design decisions.
  • Design wireframes, prototypes, and user interfaces that are visually appealing and easy to use.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to develop and refine design solutions that meet both user and business needs.
  • Conduct usability testing and gather feedback to iterate and improve designs.
  • Ensure the design is consistent with branding and adheres to UI/UX best practices.
  • Stay updated on the latest design trends, techniques, and technologies.
Qualifications:
  • Bachelor's degree in Design, Human-Computer Interaction, or a related field.
  • Proven experience as a UI/UX Designer or in a similar role.
  • Proficiency in design tools such as Adobe XD, Figma, Sketch, or similar.
  • Strong understanding of responsive and mobile-first design principles.
  • Knowledge of HTML/CSS is a plus.
  • Excellent communication and teamwork skills.
  • Attention to detail and a passion for creating seamless user experiences.
